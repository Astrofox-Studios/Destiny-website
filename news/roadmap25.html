Roadmap 2025

Hello! As we slowly get closer to Tester 5 and our first actual event, we decided that we want to share our roadmap for the next year or so. Everything in this may be subject to change and is not set in stone. (I don't think we like deadlines around here...)

Tester 5 and DG: Pilot

To start us off, we are hoping to run Tester 5 around early January. This event will stay the same format as our previous Tester events, it is basically a full test turned into a full-on event. If all goes well here, then we are aiming for later in January to do DG: Pilot, our second non-canon event. This will be our first event outside of the testing events cycle. For these 2 events there will be 6 games, which will be announced nearer to the time!
